Securite & Compliance

🔒 Authentification

JWT 15min + Refresh 7j. PIN bcrypt cost 12. Rate limit 5/60s. Lockout 15min. QR unique.

🛡 Multi-Tenant

TenantInterceptor: storeId JWT vs params/query/body. Auto-inject. ForbiddenException mismatch.

📑 Audit NF525

Hash chain SHA256 append-only. Verification GET /audit/verify. SIRET, SIREN, NAF, TVA, RCS.

🛡 RBAC

admin (tout), manager (ventes+rapports), cashier (ventes). 9 permissions granulaires.

🔐 Anti-Fraude Offline

Max 5 voids/jour, 50eur refund especes, 500eur ticket, 2 annulations consecutives. Flag 80%.

🔒 HMAC Sync

HMAC-SHA256 + nonce anti-replay + idempotency keys (500). Constant-time verify.

🛡 Rate Limiting

3 niveaux: 10/s, 100/min, 2000/h. Login specifique: 5/60s.

📑 Validation

whitelist + forbidNonWhitelisted + transform. class-validator DTOs.
